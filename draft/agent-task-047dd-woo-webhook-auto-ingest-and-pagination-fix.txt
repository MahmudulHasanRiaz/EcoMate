3) Pagination parser fix (critical UX/data visibility)
--------------------------------------------------
File: src/lib/pagination.ts

Current bug:
- Any invalid `pageSize` (e.g. 169 > 100) causes full parse failure and fallback:
  - page reset to 1
  - pageSize reset to 20
  - cursor dropped (`undefined`)

Required fix:
- Normalize each field independently.
- Clamp pageSize to `MAX_PAGE_SIZE` instead of invalidating whole payload.
- Preserve valid `cursor` even when pageSize is too high.

Expected behavior:
- `getPaginationParams({ pageSize: "169", cursor: "CUR123" })`
  -> `{ page: 1, pageSize: 100, cursor: "CUR123" }`
